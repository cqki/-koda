Škoda Kodiaq Specifications

Detailed Overview of Technical Specifications

The Škoda Kodiaq offers two robust engine options: a 2.0 TSI petrol engine delivering 190 hp and a 2.0 TDI diesel engine with 150 hp. Its dimensions are impressive, with a length of 4,697 mm, a width of 1,882 mm, and a height of 1,676 mm. In terms of capacity, the Kodiaq provides versatile boot space, offering 270 liters in the 7-seater configuration and 720 liters in the 5-seater configuration. Additionally, it features a 60-liter fuel tank, ensuring ample fuel capacity for long journeys.
